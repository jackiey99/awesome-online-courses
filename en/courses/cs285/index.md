## About this course

This is one of the most authoritative graduate-level deep RL courses. It's not an applied "tools" course — it's a research methodology course that systematically covers model-free, model-based, IRL, offline RL, meta RL, with paper-level depth on each.

## Who's it for

- Solid deep learning foundation (PyTorch + training experience)
- Aspiring RL researchers / engineers
- Anyone wanting to understand the algorithms behind RLHF, AlphaGo, robotic learning
